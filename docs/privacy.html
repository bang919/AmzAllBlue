Privacy Policy for AmzAllBlue

Last updated: July 4, 2026

AmzAllBlue is an internal business tool used by the authorized account owner to view and analyze Amazon selling, advertising, inventory, order, and product performance data.

This page is published to provide a public privacy policy URL for Amazon developer and advertising API authorization.

Information We Access

When authorized by the account owner, AmzAllBlue may access Amazon account data through Amazon APIs, including:

  • Advertising campaign performance, advertising spend, clicks, impressions, attributed orders, and attributed sales.
  • Inventory levels, FBA inventory status, product identifiers, ASINs, SKUs, and product metadata.
  • Order metrics and sales performance data needed for internal reporting.

How We Use Information

The accessed data is used only for internal reporting, analytics, inventory planning, advertising analysis, and business operations by the authorized account owner.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share Amazon account data with third parties. Data is not used for advertising networks, data brokers, or unrelated external services.

Data Storage and Security

API credentials, refresh tokens, and account data are controlled by the account owner. AmzAllBlue is intended for private internal use, and access should be limited to authorized users only.

Data Retention

Data may be retained locally or in systems controlled by the account owner for internal business analysis. The account owner may delete locally stored data at any time.

User Control

The account owner can revoke Amazon API authorization at any time through the relevant Amazon account or developer console.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the project owner through the GitHub repository: https://github.com/bang919/AmzAllBlue
